Milton is a senior Undergraduate Student in Biomedical Engineering at Tecnológico de Monterrey, México. He has previous research experience working at his university's NSF IUCRC BRAIN Center [member spotlight], in ML and DL models on wearables and biometrics (EEG, ECG, CV) using Python, Matlab and R. He joined the FNNDSC, affiliated with BCH/HMS, in Aug 2023 and is currently working with Kiho Im [member spotlight] in fetal MRI analysis. His objective is to improve the U-Net subplate segmentation model's resolution and performance.

Outside of research, he likes reading psychology books, playing chess, and rock climbing.
