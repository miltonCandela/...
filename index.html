Milton is a senior undergraduate student majoring in Biomedical Engineering at Tecnológico de Monterrey, Mexico. He conducted prior research at his university’s NSF IUCRC BRAIN Center [member spotlight], creating Machine Learning models using wearables and biometrics (EEG, ECG, Computer Vision) to predict cognitive states such as mental fatigue, STEM interest, and emotion. Currently, he collaborates with the FNNDSC [member spotlight] at Boston Children's Hospital / Harvard Medical School, developing Deep Learning models with fetal MRI data for congenital heart disease classification and cortical structure segmentation.

Outside of research, he likes reading, playing chess, and rock climbing.
