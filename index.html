Milton O. Candela-Leal

  • Research

Milton is a senior Undergraduate Student in Biomedical Engineering at Tecnológico de Monterrey, Nuevo León, México. He has previous experience at Conscious Technologies in AI models on wearables and biometrics (EEG, ECG, CV). He joined the Fetal-Neonatal Neuroimaging Developmental Science Center (FNNDSC), affiliated with Boston Children's Hospital / Harvard Medical School, in August 2023 and is currently working with Kiho Im in fetal MRI analysis. His main objective is to apply DL techniques in order to design an automated fetal diffusion MRI (dMRI) pipeline.

Outside of research, he likes reading psychology books, playing chess, and rock climbing.
